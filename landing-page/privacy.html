Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 25, 2025

Introduction

Google Watchlist Quick Open ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and discloses information about you.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users. The extension operates locally on your device.

Usage Statistics (Analytics)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the extension. This includes:

  • Extension Icon Clicks: To measure how often the extension is opened.
  • "My List" Button Clicks: To measure engagement with the search result injection feature.

This data is anonymous and does not include any Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions for specific functional purposes:

  • tabs: Used to navigate your current tab to the Google Saved page.
  • scripting: Used to inject a script into the Google Saved page to automatically click the "Search watchlist" tile.
  • storage: Used to store an anonymous client ID for analytics purposes.
  • Host Permissions (google.com): Used to inject the "My List" button into Google Search results.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties, except for the anonymous usage statistics sent to Google Analytics as described above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
